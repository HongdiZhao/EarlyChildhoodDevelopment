More About The Model

Whether the child is able to identify ten letters or not is highly correlated with if the child ever goes to early childhood education program, number of books the child has at home, and the child mother's edcuation level.

The Receiver Operating Characteristic (ROC) Curve of the model. This is a commonly used graph that summarize the performance of a classified over all possible thresholds. It is generated by plotting the True Positive Rate (y-axis) against the False Positive Rate (x-axis) as you vary the threshold for assigning observations to a given class. The Area Uder the Curve (AUC) is 0.826 (good).

ROC curves are useful even if your predicted probabilities are not "properly calibrated"; It is a useful metric even when the classess are highly unbalanced.
